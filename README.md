## What is this?

It is a self-hosted ERP backend that brings together the two most critical operations of a business — **accounting** and **inventory** — into a single, unified API.

It is inspired by the feature sets of **Zoho Books** and **Zoho Inventory**, rebuilt from the ground up using Django REST Framework, designed to be clean, extensible, and production-ready.

The goal is simple: give businesses full control over their financial and inventory data without depending on third-party SaaS platforms.
